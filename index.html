About Us

Microsoft Learn Student Chapter is a technical society of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology striving to create a community of versatile enthusiasts with a progressive mindset, promoting development holistically, mentoring and motivating its students to refine their critical thinking, logical reasoning, and coding skills. MLSC is a dynamic platform that feeds their curiosity and develops their technical skills. MLSC effectuates it by organizing numerous technology-driven events, tech meetups, and Hackathons.
